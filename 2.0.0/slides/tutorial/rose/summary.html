Rose Utilities§

Rose contains some utilities to make life easier:

rose date

$ rose date 2000 --offset '+P1Y1M1D'
2001-02-02T0000Z

$ rose date $CYLC_TASK_CYCLE_POINT --format 'The month is %B.'
The month is April.
